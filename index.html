Welcome to Dragon warrior!.
To become a dragon warrior you must defeat all the monsters and kill the boss at the end.
Each time you defeat a monster you get some gold, which you can use to buy health and weapons.
You'll get only one shot, if you get killed you need to start the process all over again!
You must kill the boss to break this curse or you'll be trapped in this loop forever!!! Good luck.
